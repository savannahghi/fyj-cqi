Welcome to the CQI published resources page for healthcare!

Here you will find a collection of resources on continuous quality improvement (CQI) as it pertains to the healthcare industry, including articles, research papers, guides, and case studies. These resources have been carefully selected to provide valuable insights and guidance on CQI best practices and techniques in the healthcare field.

CQI is a process-oriented approach to improving the quality of products, services, and processes. It involves identifying areas for improvement, collecting and analyzing data, and implementing changes to achieve desired outcomes. In the healthcare industry, CQI is often used to improve patient care, reduce errors, and increase efficiency.

We hope that these resources will help you to learn more about CQI and how it can be applied to improve the quality of healthcare in your organization. Whether you are new to CQI or an experienced practitioner in the healthcare industry, we hope that you will find something of value in these resources.

Thank you for visiting our page, and we hope that you find it useful.
