About Us

The CQI Platform for HIV Services is a collaborative initiative developed by USAID and the Ministry of Health to enhance the quality of HIV care across Kenya. Our mission is to empower healthcare providers with cutting-edge tools, resources, and data-driven insights to improve patient outcomes and drive continuous quality improvement in HIV services.

Since our inception in 2018, we've been working closely with healthcare facilities, NGOs, and government agencies to streamline HIV care processes, enhance data quality, and foster a culture of continuous improvement. Our platform serves as a central hub for CQI projects, data analysis, and knowledge sharing, connecting thousands of healthcare professionals across the country.
